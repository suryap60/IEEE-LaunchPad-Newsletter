Introduction

Welcome to IEEE LaunchPad Kerala! Our mission is to empower and inspire the next generation of innovators and engineers. We provide a platform for students and professionals to collaborate, learn, and grow through various events, workshops, and projects.

One of the most prestigious event of IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn that is determined to serve the goal of creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates.

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier job fair that brings together talented individuals and innovative companies in the technical and engineering fields. This collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn aims to create meaningful connections that drive progress and innovation in Kerala's job market.

Important Update: Online Aptitude Test and Mock Test Details

Dear Participant,

We hope this message finds you well. Launchpad Kerala is entering an exciting new phase, and we're thrilled to invite you to the next stage of our selection process.

Online Aptitude Test Details:

The online aptitude test is scheduled for July 4th, and participation is mandatory for all candidates aiming to proceed to the interview round. Your performance in this test, along with your technical challenge results, will determine your position in the final rank list. Interviews will be conducted in Trivandrum and Kochi based on this ranking.

Mock Test Preparation:

To help you prepare effectively, we will be conducting a mock test on July 3rd. Further details regarding this will be shared in a separate email soon.

Eligibility Criteria:

Please note that only those who have completed at least one task in the Technical Challenge by July 1st will be eligible to participate in the aptitude test. If you haven't started the technical challenge yet, we encourage you to begin at your earliest convenience. You can find detailed instructions on how to start your technical challenges here.

Act Now:

Remember, the deadline to start your technical challenge is July 1st. This is a crucial step towards launching your career with Launchpad Kerala. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your skills and potential.

We are excited about your participation and look forward to seeing you excel in the upcoming challenges. Should you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Event Roadmap

Here is the detailed roadmap for the upcoming events:

  1. Online Registration on Launchpad Website: Register online to begin your journey with IEEE LaunchPad Kerala.
  2. Confirmation: Receive a confirmation email and your Launchpad Kerala ID.
  3. Join the Community: Join the Gtech Mulearn Discord Server and complete the Launchpad Challenges. Details can be found here.
  4. Technical Challenge: Participate in various technical challenges available in the Discord Server under the #ieee-launchpad-announcement channel.
  5. Online Aptitude Test: Take the online aptitude test, which is scheduled for July 4th. This test is mandatory for all candidates proceeding to the interview round.
  6. Await the Finalized Results: Results will be based on your performance in the aptitude test and the technical challenges.
  7. Final Interview: Attend the final interview conducted in Trivandrum and Kochi based on the ranking.
