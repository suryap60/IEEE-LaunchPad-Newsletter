Important Update: Online Aptitude Test and Mock Test Details

Dear Participant,

We hope this message finds you well. Launchpad Kerala is entering an exciting new phase, and we're thrilled to invite you to the next stage of our selection process.

Online Aptitude Test Details:

The online aptitude test is scheduled for July 4th, and participation is mandatory for all candidates aiming to proceed to the interview round. Your performance in this test, along with your technical challenge results, will determine your position in the final rank list. Interviews will be conducted in Trivandrum and Kochi based on this ranking.

Mock Test Preparation:

To help you prepare effectively, we will be conducting a mock test on July 3rd. Further details regarding this will be shared in a separate email soon.

Eligibility Criteria:

Please note that only those who have completed at least one task in the Technical Challenge by July 1st will be eligible to participate in the aptitude test. If you haven't started the technical challenge yet, we encourage you to begin at your earliest convenience. You can find detailed instructions on how to start your technical challenges here.

Act Now:

Remember, the deadline to start your technical challenge is July 1st. This is a crucial step towards launching your career with Launchpad Kerala. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your skills and potential.

We are excited about your participation and look forward to seeing you excel in the upcoming challenges. Should you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out.
